Rain

Stablecoin powered finance

Rain is the global stablecoin infrastructure platform for enterprises, neobanks, platforms, and developers. As a Visa Principal Member, Rain issues cards that work anywhere Visa is accepted, powering millions of purchases in over 150 countries. The company enables businesses to integrate stablecoin-powered payments into their products, bridging the gap between decentralized finance and everyday commerce.
