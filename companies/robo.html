Robo

A standardized robot arm for teachable automation

Robo is building a standardized robot arm platform for teachable automation. Their hardware and software stack enables businesses to deploy robot arms that can be taught new tasks through demonstration rather than programming, making industrial automation accessible to non-technical operators.
